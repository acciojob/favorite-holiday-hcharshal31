Mahabaleshwar


Mahabaleshwar, nestled in the Sahyadri mountain range of Maharashtra, India, is a picturesque hill station renowned for its lush greenery, stunning viewpoints, and strawberry farms. Here are the top five places to visit and foods to try while in Mahabaleshwar:

  1. Arthur's Seat
  2. Pratapgad Fort
  3. Venna Lake
  4. Mapro Garden
  5. Elephant's Head Point
  • Strawberry Delicacies
  • Corn Patties
  • Puran Poli
